The Nintendo Switch: what do you make of it?

Posted 08/03/2017

By now you should know that there is a new console that has hit the market, the Nintendo Switch; the one console you can take everywhere. For months and months we all waited to indulge in the nostalgia of Zelda on the Nintendo Switch and expereince the new console in action. The Nintendo Switch is a "hybrid" console which can be used on your TV at home but also on your travels, improving the past transported consoles such as the DS and Gameboy. Transitioning between these two modes is pretty simple as the click of two buttons registers the screen change. With its complex design, placing the switch into the dock provided will turn the system on.

The specifications for the Nintendo Switch are:

  • Processor: Nvidia Tegra Custom Processor
  • Storage: 32GB, expandable through MicroSD cards up to 2TB
  • Screen: 720p, 6.2-inch LCD screen, capacitive multi-touch
  • Resolution: Up to 1080p on TVs
  • Audio: Stereo speakers
  • Ports: (On Dock) 3 USB Ports, AC Port, HDMI Port
  • Battery: 2.5-6.5 hours of playtime. Lithium-ion 4310mAh
  • Charging Time: Three hours in sleep mode
  • Controller Battery: 20 hours of playtime
  • Controller Charging Time: 3.5 hours
  • Wireless on console: Bluetooth 4.1
  • Wireless on controllers: Bluetooth 3.0

2017, what do you have in store for us?

Posted 01/03/2017

New year, New Games and my god are we excited about them. This year, there are some imense games from blockbusters to potential under-the-radar gems to look forward to for Xbox, Playstation and PC. When viewing the video game industry, 2017 is off to a brilliant start. Researching and shifting through the titles, here are some of the games that 2017 has to offer:

    Nier: Automata:
  • Platforms: PS4, PC
  • Release Date: March 7 2017
  • Description: Nier: Automata blends Bayonetta with Battlestar Galactica, fusing the kind of hyperactive third person combat PlatinumGames is best known for. Millennia, a hostile alien force has sent legions of robots to invade earth, forcing humanity to evacuate to the moon. However, humanity decides to fight back in a grim sci-fi setting adventure.
    • Tom Clancy's Ghost Recon Wildlands
  • Platforms: PS4, Xbox One, PC
  • Release Date: March 7 2017
  • Description: Ubisoft are taking advantage of their biggest strengths- open-world sandboxes and Tom Clancy games- and combining them to make a cartel based entry into the Ghost Recon series. Ghost Recon Wildlands deploys you and your squad into vast deserts, mountain ranges and extreme swamps in the hope of destroying the cartel. The tactical troop shooting will take place in Bolivia, located in the South Of America, where a cocaine cartel called 'Santa Blanca' has seized all power.
